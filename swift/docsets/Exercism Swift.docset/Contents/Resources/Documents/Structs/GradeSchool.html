GradeSchool

public struct GradeSchool

Store students’ names along with the grade they are in.

Usage:

  • Add a student’s name to the roster for a grade
    • Add Jim to grade 2.
    • OK.
  • Get a list of all students enrolled in a grade
    • Which students are in grade 2?
    • We’ve only got Jim just now.
  • Get a sorted list of all students in all grades. Grades should sort as 1, 2, 3, etc., and students within a grade should be sorted alphabetically by name.
    • Who all is enrolled in school right now?
    • Grade 1: Anna, Barb, and Charlie. Grade 2: Alex, Peter and Zoe. Grade 3â€¦

This exercise was inspired by a pairing session with Phil Battos at gSchool.

Remark

All our students only have one name. (It’s a small town. What do you want?)
